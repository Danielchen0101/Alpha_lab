# Code of Conduct

AlphaLab contributors should be able to discuss technical and product decisions without hostility, harassment, or personal attacks.

## Expected behavior

- Be specific about code, evidence, and trade-offs.
- Assume good intent while remaining clear about risk.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy, especially around financial accounts and credentials.
- Give credit for ideas and work.
- Accept review feedback professionally and keep disagreements focused on the work.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate exposure of private data, sustained disruption, and personal insults are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close conversations, restrict participation, or ban an account when needed to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, with attention to the safety of the person reporting.

This policy applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
